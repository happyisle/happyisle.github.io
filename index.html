Welcome

Helen is an organist and mezzo-soprano playing and singing in churches
in and around the Greater Glasgow and Renfrewshire area.

She can be heard every weekend at St Andrew's Metropolitan Cathedral
and the church of St Alphonsus in Glasgow.

She is available to sing or play for weddings, funerals
and all other church services.

Weddings

Music is a wonderful way to make your wedding ceremony truly personal.
From a psalm or hymn that resonates with you, to a piece or song that means something extra special, Helen has the experience to guide you through selecting the perfect music for your special day. She will even specially arrange a piece for organ for you if requested.

Previous arrangements have included Paul Mounsey's Red River, Joaquin Prieto's The Wedding, and a Wedding March based on the carol Joy to the World.

Helen's work as a wedding organist and singer has taken her to some beautiful locations in Glasgow, as well as places as far flung as the Floral Hall in Buxton and St Magnus Cathedral, Orkney.

To make a booking or a general enquiry about weddings, contact Helen here.

Funerals

Music is always important at funerals. The right music can help make a funeral just the sought of loving occasion your loved one would want.

In the past, Helen has helped people track down a favourite piece or hymn that really helps call that special person to mind. Previous hymn requests have included May is the Month of Mary, The Sun is Shining Brightly, and You Raise Me Up.

Sometimes extended family and friends may not know some of the hymns or music for a funeral service. Having an organist who can also lead the congregation in singing can be a real benefit. Helen has extensive experience of singing from the organ, with or without a microphone as needs be.

As a trained cantor, Helen can lead the singing of any psalm you choose to go with your selected readings, from old favourites like The Lord's My Shepherd, to modern classics like On Eagles' Wings, as well as more unusual choices.

To make a booking or a general enquiry about funerals, contact Helen here.

Regular Engagements
and other Services

Helen Alexander is the Director of Music at St Alphonsus in Calton
and an organist at St Andrew's Metropolitan Cathedral.

Helen currently has vacancies for regular engagements on Saturday and Sunday evening, early on Sunday morning, and any weekday. She can travel anywhere in the Glasgow, Motherwell and Paisley dioceses. Engagements for one-off services and deputisations for other organists are also welcome.

CV and references can be provided on request. Helen is a member of the PVG scheme.

To ask Helen about an engagement or to make a general enquiry, contact Helen here.
